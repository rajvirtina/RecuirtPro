# RecuirtPro Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** October 24, 2025  
**Last Updated:** October 24, 2025  
**Version:** 1.0

## Introduction

RecuirtPro is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and protect your personal information during the interview process. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the RecuirtPro platform, including our desktop monitoring application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, company name
- **Profile Information:** Resume/CV, work history, education, skills, certifications
- **Authentication Data:** Encrypted passwords, two-factor authentication codes, session tokens
- **Communication Records:** Messages, emails, notifications sent through the platform

### 1.2 Interview Data
- **Scheduling Information:** Interview dates, times, timezones, duration
- **Meeting Details:** Video conference links, meeting IDs, calendar integrations
- **Assessment Data:** Interview questions, responses, evaluations, scores
- **Recording Data:** Video/audio recordings (only when explicitly consented)

### 1.3 Proctoring & Monitoring Data

When you use our desktop monitoring application during interviews, we collect:

#### System Information
- Operating system type and version
- Hardware specifications (CPU, RAM, display count)
- Network information (IP address, connection quality)
- Browser type and version

#### Activity Monitoring
- **Running Processes:** Names of active applications and processes
- **Window Activity:** Titles of active windows and window focus changes
- **Display Configuration:** Number of connected monitors, screen resolution
- **Screen Sharing Status:** Whether screen sharing is active
- **System Resources:** CPU and memory usage metrics

#### Behavioral Data
- Mouse movements and click patterns
- Keyboard activity indicators (not keystrokes themselves)
- Tab switching and window switching events
- Browser activity (multiple browser instances)
- Face detection and gaze tracking (when video proctoring is enabled)

### 1.4 Technical & Usage Data
- Device information and unique identifiers
- Log files, error reports, crash data
- Usage statistics and feature interactions
- Performance metrics and loading times

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Primary Purposes
- **Interview Administration:** Schedule, conduct, and manage interviews
- **Candidate Assessment:** Evaluate qualifications and suitability for positions
- **Communication:** Send notifications, updates, and interview-related messages
- **Account Management:** Create, maintain, and secure user accounts

### 2.2 Proctoring & Integrity
- **Cheating Detection:** Identify unauthorized applications, multiple browsers, or suspicious behavior
- **Fair Assessment:** Ensure equal treatment and consistent interview conditions
- **Violation Reporting:** Generate reports of detected policy violations for HR review
- **Quality Assurance:** Monitor system performance and interview quality

### 2.3 Platform Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
- Identify and fix bugs, errors, and security vulnerabilities
- Develop new features based on user needs
- Optimize performance and reliability

### 2.4 Legal & Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Respond to legal requests and prevent fraud
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect rights, safety, and security of users

## 3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

### 3.1 Within the Platform
- **Hiring Companies:** Share candidate information with employers you've applied to
- **Interviewers:** Share interview details with designated panel members
- **HR Personnel:** Provide access to hiring managers and HR administrators

### 3.2 Third-Party Service Providers
We may share data with trusted partners who assist us:
- **Cloud Infrastructure:** AWS, Google Cloud, Azure for hosting
- **Email Services:** SendGrid, Mailgun for transactional emails
- **Video Conferencing:** Integration with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics, Mixpanel (anonymized data only)
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe, PayPal for subscription payments

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when:
- Required by law, court order, or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights or property
- To prevent fraud, security issues, or technical problems
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

### 3.4 What We Never Share
- We **never sell** your personal information to third parties
- We **never share** raw monitoring data with advertisers
- We **never** publicly disclose individual interview performance without consent

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Active Account Data
- Account information: Retained while your account is active
- Application data: Retained for the duration of the hiring process + 12 months
- Interview recordings: Retained for 90 days unless extended by company policy

### 4.2 Proctoring & Monitoring Data
- **Proctoring Events:** Retained for **90 days** after interview completion
- **Violation Reports:** Retained for **12 months** for compliance purposes
- **Aggregate Statistics:** Retained indefinitely in anonymized form

### 4.3 Deleted Account Data
- Personal information: Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
- Interview records: Anonymized (name removed) but kept for company records
- Violation logs: Anonymized but retained for audit purposes

### 4.4 Legal Hold
Data may be retained longer if subject to legal obligations, disputes, or investigations.

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 Access & Portability (GDPR Article 15, 20)
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
- **Data Portability:** Export your data in machine-readable format (JSON, CSV)
- **How to Request:** Email privacy@recruitpro.com with subject "Data Access Request"

### 5.2 Correction & Deletion (GDPR Article 16, 17)
- **Right to Rectify:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **How to Request:** Email privacy@recruitpro.com or use in-app settings

### 5.3 Object & Restrict (GDPR Article 18, 21)
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Right to Restrict:** Limit how we process your data during disputes
- **How to Request:** Contact privacy@recruitpro.com

### 5.4 Withdraw Consent (GDPR Article 7)
- You may withdraw consent for monitoring at any time
- **Note:** Withdrawal may result in disqualification from the interview process
- **How:** Click "Stop Monitoring" in the desktop app or contact HR

### 5.5 Lodge a Complaint
- You have the right to file a complaint with your local data protection authority
- **EU:** https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en
- **UK:** Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
- **US:** Contact the FTC or your state Attorney General

## 6. Security Measures

### 6.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS 1.3 and WSS
- **Encryption at Rest:** Database encryption using AES-256
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access control (RBAC) and least privilege principle
- **Authentication:** Bcrypt password hashing, optional 2FA, session management

### 6.2 Organizational Safeguards
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Employee training on data protection and privacy
- Data breach response plan with notification procedures
- Third-party vendor security assessments

### 6.3 Monitoring Security
- Desktop app uses secure API communication with JWT tokens
- Monitoring data encrypted before transmission
- No raw keystroke logging or screen capture
- Violation detection uses local processing when possible

## 7. Children's Privacy

RecuirtPro is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.
