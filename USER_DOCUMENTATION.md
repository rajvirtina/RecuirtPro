## Candidate User Guide

### Overview
As a candidate, you'll use RecruiterPro to complete interview processes for job opportunities. This guide walks you through registration, interview participation, and account management.

### 1. Getting Started

#### 1.1 Account Registration

**Step 1: Access the Platform**
- Open your browser and navigate to the RecruiterPro web application
- Click the **"Sign Up"** button on the login page
- Select **"Candidate"** from the role options

**Step 2: Enter Your Information**
Fill out the registration form with:
- **First Name** - Your given name
- **Last Name** - Your family name
- **Email Address** - A valid email you have access to (used for all communications)
- **Password** - Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters, includes uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters)
- **Confirm Password** - Re-enter your password to confirm

**Step 3: Privacy & Consent**
- Read the Privacy Policy (linked on the form)
- Check the box to accept Terms of Service
- **Consent Modal**: A consent dialog will appear asking for permissions:
  - ✓ Data Processing & Platform Usage
  - ✓ Interview Recording (if applicable)
  - ✓ Candidate Tracking
  - Review each item and click **"Accept"** to proceed, or **"Decline"** to reject
  - You can change these preferences later in your account settings

**Step 4: Verify Your Email**
- A verification email will be sent to your registered email address
- Click the **"Verify Email"** link in the email
- Once verified, you'll be redirected to the login page

**Step 5: Complete Your Profile**
- Log in with your email and password
- You'll be directed to the **Profile Setup** page
- Add optional information:
  - **Profile Photo** - Upload a professional photo
  - **Phone Number** - For interview coordination
  - **Location** - Your current city/country
  - **LinkedIn Profile** - Optional link to your profile
  - **Resume** - Upload your latest resume (PDF or DOC)
  - **Skills** - Add skills relevant to jobs you're applying for
  - **Work Experience** - Brief summary of your background

- Click **"Save Profile"** to complete setup

### 2. Browsing & Applying for Jobs

#### 2.1 View Available Jobs

**Step 1: Access Jobs Page**
- Log in to your account
- Click **"Jobs"** in the main navigation menu
- You'll see a list of open positions

**Step 2: Filter & Search**
- **Search Bar** - Type keywords (job title, company name, location)
- **Filters** - Narrow down by:
  - Job Category
  - Location
  - Experience Level
  - Salary Range
  - Company
- Click **"Apply Filter"** to see matching results

**Step 3: View Job Details**
- Click on any job title to open the detailed job posting
- Review:
  - Job Title & Description
  - Qualifications Required
  - Responsibilities
  - Salary Range (if available)
  - Application Deadline
  - Company Information
  - Interview Process Details

#### 2.2 Apply for a Job

**Step 1: Click Apply**
- On the job details page, click the **"Apply Now"** button
- A dialog will appear with application details

**Step 2: Review Pre-filled Information**
- Your name, email, and phone are auto-populated
- Review your attached resume (you can change it here)
- Add a cover letter or optional message to the employer

**Step 3: Submit Application**
- Click **"Submit Application"**
- You'll receive a confirmation message with your application ID
- A confirmation email will be sent to your registered email

**Step 4: Track Application Status**
- Go to **"My Interviews"** or **"Applications"** in the sidebar
- View all your applications with statuses:
  - 🔵 **Submitted** - Application received, under review
  - 🟡 **Under Review** - Employer reviewing your application
  - 🟢 **Interview Scheduled** - You've been selected for an interview
  - 🔴 **Rejected** - Application was not selected
  - ✅ **Offer Extended** - Job offer made

### 3. Interview Process

#### 3.1 Interview Invitation

**Step 1: Receive Invitation**
- When an employer selects your application, you'll receive an email with:
  - Interview date and time
  - Interview format (Video, Phone, or In-Person)
  - Interviewer name and contact information
  - Meeting link (for video interviews)

**Step 2: Accept/Decline Interview**
- Log in to your account
- Go to **"My Interviews"**
- Find the pending interview
- Click **"Accept"** or **"Decline"**
- If you decline, specify a reason (optional)

**Step 3: Add to Calendar**
- Click **"Add to Calendar"**
- Your calendar application (Google, Outlook, etc.) will open with the interview details pre-filled
- Click **"Add"** to save it to your calendar
- A reminder will be set for 1 hour before the interview

#### 3.2 Prepare for Video Interview

**Before the Interview (24 hours prior):**
- Click on the interview in **"My Interviews"**
- Click **"Join Interview"** button
- If this is your first time, you'll see the **Consent Check Screen**:
  - Verify your camera and microphone are working
  - You may be asked to grant permissions to your browser
  - Click **"Allow"** when browser asks for camera/microphone access
  - Take a test photo/video to confirm video quality
  - Ensure your internet connection is stable (minimum 5 Mbps recommended)
  - Check your background - keep it professional
  - Ensure good lighting (face is clearly visible)

**Prepare Your Environment:**
- Close all unnecessary browser tabs and applications
- Silence your phone
- Notify anyone in your location not to disturb you
- Have your resume and any notes nearby (off-camera)
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection

#### 3.3 Join the Interview

**Step 1: Click Join Interview**
- At the scheduled interview time, log in to your account
- Go to **"My Interviews"**
- Click the **"Join Interview"** button
- You'll be taken to the **Proctoring Check Page**

**Step 2: Complete Proctoring Check**
- The system will verify:
  - Your camera is working
  - Your microphone is operational
  - You're using a supported browser
  - Your internet connection is stable
- Click **"Verify & Continue"** once all checks pass
- Accept any browser permission requests

**Step 3: Enter Interview Room**
- You'll be placed in a virtual waiting room
- Your video feed will be visible
- The interviewer(s) will join shortly
- Wait for the interview to begin (typically 1-2 minutes after scheduled time)

**Step 4: During Interview**
- Look at the camera when speaking (makes eye contact with interviewer)
- Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
- If you need clarification, ask questions
- Take notes if helpful (interviewer can see your movement)
- Interview typically lasts 30-45 minutes
- Audio and video will be recorded (as per your consent)

**Step 5: End Interview**
- Once complete, the interviewer will end the session
- You'll see a **"Interview Complete"** message
- Click **"Return to Dashboard"** to go back to your profile
- A summary email will be sent within 24 hours

#### 3.4 Interview Options by Type

**Video Interview:**
- Most common format
- Uses your device's camera and microphone
- Follow steps 3.2 and 3.3 above

**Phone Interview:**
- Interviewer will call your registered phone number at the scheduled time
- Ensure your phone is charged
- Find a quiet location
- Be ready 5 minutes before the scheduled time

**In-Person Interview:**
- You'll receive location details and directions in the invitation
- Arrive 10 minutes early
- Bring a copy of your resume
- Dress professionally

**Step 2: Edit Information**

**Step 3: Save Changes**

**Step 1: Access Notification Settings**

**Step 2: Configure Preferences**
- Email Notifications:
  - ☑ Interview Invitations
  - ☑ Application Updates
  - ☑ New Job Matches
  - ☑ Reminders (1 hour before interview)
- In-App Notifications:
  - ☑ Enable In-App Alerts
  - ☑ Desktop Notifications

**Step 3: Save**
- Click **"Save Preferences"**

#### 4.3 Change Password

**Step 1: Access Security Settings**
- Go to **"Settings"** → **"Security"** → **"Change Password"**

**Step 2: Enter Current & New Password**
- Enter your **Current Password**
- Enter your **New Password** (must be different and meet complexity requirements)
- Confirm **New Password**

**Step 3: Update**
- Click **"Update Password"**
- You'll be logged out and must log in with your new password

#### 4.4 View Consent History

**Step 1: Access Privacy Settings**
- Go to **"Settings"** → **"Privacy"** → **"Consent History"**

**Step 2: Review Consents**
- You'll see all consents you've given with:
  - Consent Type (Data Processing, Recording, Tracking, etc.)
  - Date Given
  - Status (Active/Withdrawn)
  - Version of consent form accepted

**Step 3: Withdraw Consent (Optional)**
- Click **"Withdraw"** next to any consent
- Confirm in the dialog
- Your consent will be revoked immediately

#### 4.5 Download Personal Data (GDPR Right to Access)

**Step 1: Request Data Export**
- Go to **"Settings"** → **"Privacy"** → **"Download My Data"**

**Step 2: Review Data to Export**
- System shows what data will be included:
  - Account Information
  - Profile Data
  - Application History
  - Interview Records
  - Consent Logs
  - Proctoring Records

**Step 3: Request Export**
- Click **"Request Export"**
- You'll receive an email with a download link within 24 hours
- The file will be in JSON format

#### 4.6 Delete Your Account (GDPR Right to Erasure)

**Step 1: Access Account Deletion**
- Go to **"Settings"** → **"Privacy"** → **"Delete Account"**

**Step 2: Review Implications**
- Read the warning message about account deletion
- Understand what data will be deleted
- Review if you have any pending interviews

**Step 3: Confirm Deletion**
- Enter your password to confirm
- Check the **"I understand this is permanent"** checkbox
- Click **"Delete Account"**

**Step 4: Final Confirmation**
- An email will be sent asking you to confirm deletion
- Click the **"Confirm Deletion"** link in the email
- Your account and all personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

### 5. Interview Results & Feedback

#### 5.1 Check Interview Status

**Step 1: View Interview**
- Go to **"My Interviews"**
- Find the interview you completed

**Step 2: Check Status**
- ✅ **Completed** - Interview finished, waiting for feedback
- 🔍 **Under Review** - Interviewer evaluating your performance
- 💬 **Feedback Available** - You can read interviewer's feedback
- ✔️ **Passed** - You've moved to next round
- ❌ **Not Selected** - You didn't move forward

#### 5.2 Read Feedback (if provided)

- Click the interview to expand details
- If feedback is available, you'll see:
  - Overall Score
  - Strengths Highlighted
  - Areas for Improvement
  - Interviewer Comments
  - Next Steps (if applicable)

#### 5.3 Receive Offer or Rejection

- **Offer**: You'll receive an email with job offer details, including:
  - Position Title
  - Start Date
  - Compensation
  - Benefits
  - Next Steps (signing documents, etc.)

- **Rejection**: You'll receive a rejection email. You may:
  - Ask for feedback (if option is available)
  - Apply for other positions
  - Contact HR if you have questions

## HR Manager User Guide

### Overview
As an HR Manager, you'll use RecruiterPro to create job postings, manage candidates, schedule interviews, and make hiring decisions. This guide covers the complete hiring workflow.

### 1. Getting Started

#### 1.1 Account Registration

**Step 1: Access the Platform**
- Open your browser and navigate to the RecruiterPro web application
- Click **"Sign Up"** → Select **"HR Manager"**

**Step 2: Enter Company & Personal Information**
- **Company Name** - Your organization's name
- **Company Industry** - Select from dropdown
- **Company Size** - Number of employees
- **Your First Name** - Your given name
- **Your Last Name** - Your family name
- **Email** - Your work email address
- **Password** - Create a strong password

**Step 3: Verify Email & Complete Setup**
- Check your email for verification link
- Click to verify
- Log in to access the HR Dashboard

#### 1.2 Complete HR Profile

**Step 1: Access Profile Setup**
- You'll be guided to the **HR Profile Setup** page
- Fill in:
  - **Department** - Which department you're hiring for (HR, Engineering, Sales, etc.)
  - **Phone Number** - Your contact number
  - **Office Location** - Physical office address
  - **Team Members** - Add other HR managers/interviewers who'll use the system

**Step 2: Add Team Members**
- Click **"Add Team Member"**
- Enter their email address
- Select their role:
  - Interviewer (can schedule and conduct interviews)
  - HR Assistant (can manage applications, view reports)
  - Department Head (can approve postings and make final hiring decisions)
- Click **"Send Invitation"**
- They'll receive an email to join your company's account

**Step 3: Save**
- Click **"Complete Setup"**

### 2. Creating Job Postings

#### 2.1 Post a New Job

**Step 1: Access Job Posting Page**
- Log in to HR Dashboard
- Click **"Jobs"** → **"Create New Job"**

**Step 2: Enter Job Details**

**Basic Information:**
- **Job Title** - Position title (e.g., "Senior Software Engineer")
- **Department** - Which department (pre-filled if applicable)
- **Location** - Where the job is based (city/country)
- **Employment Type** - Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Internship
- **Experience Level** - Entry, Mid, Senior, Lead
- **Salary Range** - Minimum and Maximum (optional, can be hidden from candidates)

**Job Description:**
- **Summary** - 1-2 sentence overview
- **About Your Company** - Background and culture
- **Responsibilities** - 5-10 key responsibilities (can use rich text, bullet points)
- **Required Qualifications** - Must-have skills and experience
- **Preferred Qualifications** - Nice-to-have skills
- **Benefits** - Health insurance, PTO, remote work options, etc.

**Step 3: Configure Interview Process**

- **Number of Rounds** - How many interview rounds (typically 1-4)
- **Round 1 Details:**
  - Format (Video, Phone, In-Person)
  - Estimated Duration (in minutes)
  - Interviewer(s) - Select from your team
  - Questions/Template - Choose from existing templates or create new
  
- **Add Additional Rounds** - Click "Add Round" for additional interview stages

**Step 4: Set Screening Questions**

- **Add Screen Question** - Click to add questions
- For each question:
  - Enter the question text
  - Select type: Multiple Choice, Short Answer, Essay, File Upload
  - Make required or optional
  - Set time limit (for recorded answers if applicable)

**Example questions:**
- "Why are you interested in this role?"
- "What's your experience with [specific technology/skill]?"
- "Tell us about your greatest professional achievement"

**Step 5: Configure Proctoring (if video interview)

- **Enable Proctoring** - Toggle on for proctored interviews
- **Recording Settings:**
  - ☑ Record Audio
  - ☑ Record Video
  - ☑ Record Screen
- **Monitoring Requirements:**
  - Require stable internet
  - Require proper lighting
  - Require full-screen mode
  - Require face detection

**Step 6: Review & Publish**

- Review all information
- Click **"Preview as Candidate"** to see how job posting looks to candidates
- Click **"Publish Job"**
- Job becomes visible to candidates immediately

#### 2.2 Edit Job Posting

**Step 1: Access Job**
- Go to **"Jobs"** → Find the job in the list
- Click **"Edit"** or the job title

**Step 2: Modify Details**
- Update any information (same fields as creation)
- Changes take effect immediately

**Step 3: Pause/Archive Job**
- Click **"Pause Job"** to temporarily stop accepting applications
- Click **"Archive Job"** to remove from active listings

### 3. Managing Candidates & Applications

#### 3.1 View Applications

**Step 1: Access Applications**
- Go to **"Dashboard"** → **"Applications"**
- Or click a specific job to see applications for that job

**Step 2: Filter & Search**
- **Status Filter:**
  - New/Unreviewed
  - Shortlisted
  - Rejected
  - Interview Scheduled
  - Offer Extended
  - Hired
  - Withdrawn
  
- **Search** - Find candidate by name or email
- **Sort** - By date, score, or status

**Step 3: View Candidate Application**
- Click on candidate name
- Review:
  - Resume (view/download)
  - Cover Letter
  - Screening Question Responses
  - Application Date
  - Score (if auto-scored)

#### 3.2 Screen & Rank Candidates

**Step 1: Review Application**
- Open candidate's application

**Step 2: Score & Evaluate**
- **Scoring Options:**
  - Star Rating (1-5 stars)
  - Percentage Score (0-100%)
  - Rubric-based (if template exists)
  
- Evaluate based on:
  - Required qualifications match
  - Experience level
  - Response quality to screening questions
  - Resume quality

**Step 3: Take Notes**
- Add private notes visible only to your team
- Document reasons for your assessment
- Example: "Strong Python skills, limited React experience"

**Step 4: Update Status**
- Click **"Change Status"**
- Options:
  - **Shortlist** - Move to next round
  - **Reject** - Decline this candidate
  - **Hold** - Keep for future consideration
  - **Interview Ready** - Move to interview scheduling

**Step 5: Save**
- Click **"Save Assessment"**
- Changes are recorded with timestamp and username

#### 3.3 Send Rejection

**Step 1: Select Candidate**
- Find candidate in applications
- Click **"Reject"** or status **"Rejected"**

**Step 2: Send Rejection Email**
- System shows pre-filled rejection email template
- Customize message if desired
- Click **"Send Rejection"**
- Email is sent immediately to candidate

#### 3.4 Export Candidate Data

**Step 1: Select Candidates**
- Go to **"Applications"**
- Select candidates using checkboxes (or "Select All")

**Step 2: Export**
- Click **"Export"**
- Choose format: CSV or PDF
- Click **"Download"**
- File will contain selected candidate information

### 4. Scheduling Interviews

#### 4.1 Schedule Individual Interview

**Step 1: Access Scheduling**
- Go to candidate's application
- Click **"Schedule Interview"**

**Step 2: Select Interview Round**
- Choose which round (Round 1, 2, etc.)
- Confirm interview details (format, duration, questions)

**Step 3: Set Interview Details**

- **Date & Time:**
  - Click calendar to select date
  - Select time (shows available time slots)
  - System shows interviewer availability

- **Interviewer(s):**
  - Select from your team
  - Can add multiple interviewers
  - Shows their availability

- **Meeting Link (for video):**
  - System auto-generates or you can add custom link
  - Link will be shared with candidate

- **Timezone:**
  - Select candidate's timezone (auto-detect if available)
  - System shows local time conversions

**Step 4: Send Invitation**
- Add optional message to candidate
- Click **"Schedule Interview"**
- Email sent to candidate with:
  - Interview date/time
  - Format details
  - Meeting link
  - Interviewer information
  - Request to accept/decline

- Email sent to interviewer with:
  - Interview time
  - Candidate information
  - Interview questions/template
  - Meeting link
  - Candidate resume

#### 4.2 Bulk Interview Scheduling

**Step 1: Select Multiple Candidates**
- Go to **"Applications"**
- Select multiple candidates using checkboxes
- Click **"Bulk Actions"** → **"Schedule Interviews"**

**Step 2: Set Common Details**
- Interview Round (same for all)
- Interviewer(s) (same for all)
- Duration

**Step 3: Choose Scheduling Method**
- **Option A: Time Slots** - Assign candidates to pre-set time slots
- **Option B: Candidate Preference** - Send scheduling link for candidates to choose time
- **Option C: Automatic** - System finds optimal times based on availability

**Step 4: Send Invitations**
- Review scheduling
- Click **"Schedule All"**
- All candidates receive invitations

#### 4.3 Reschedule Interview

**Step 1: Find Interview**
- Go to **"Dashboard"** → **"Interviews"**
- Or find via candidate application
- Find the interview to reschedule

**Step 2: Reschedule**
- Click **"Reschedule"**
- Select new date/time
- Add reason for reschedule (optional)

**Step 3: Notify Parties**
- Click **"Send Notifications"**
- Emails sent to candidate and interviewer(s) with new time
- Calendar invitations updated

#### 4.4 Cancel Interview

**Step 1: Find Interview**
- Locate the interview in candidates or dashboard

**Step 2: Cancel**
- Click **"Cancel Interview"**
- Provide cancellation reason

**Step 3: Notify Parties**
- Click **"Notify Parties"**
- Email sent to candidate and interviewer explaining cancellation
- Option to reschedule offered

  - Meeting link

**Step 3: Start Interview (if you're interviewing)**

- System confirms camera/audio

#### 5.2 Access Interview Recording & Transcript

**Step 2: Review Recording**

**Step 3: View Transcript (if available)**

**Step 3: Score Interview**

**Step 4: Save**
- Click **"Save Notes"**

#### 5.4 Interview Feedback & Calibration

**Step 1: View Team Feedback**

**Step 2: Calibration Meeting**

**Step 3: Make Hiring Decision**
- After discussion, return to candidate

- Options: Hire, Reject, Hold for Future

- **Start Date** - When they'll begin
- **Salary** - Annual or hourly rate
- **Benefits:**
  - ☑ Health Insurance (type)
  - ☑ 401k (percentage match)
  - ☑ PTO (days/hours)
  - ☑ Remote Work (options)
  - Add other benefits specific to your company

- **Offer Letter Text:**
  - Edit template or add custom message
  - Include contingencies (background check, reference check)
  - Include offer deadline

**Step 3: Attach Documents**
- Add offer letter PDF
- Add employee handbook
- Add other relevant documents

**Step 4: Send Offer**
- Click **"Send Offer"**

  - Offer details
  - Offer letter
  - Instructions to accept/decline
  - Deadline to respond

**Step 5: Track Offer Status**

### 5. Troubleshooting

#### 5.1 Camera Not Working

**Issue:** Camera not detected or shows black screen

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Permissions:**
   - Windows: Settings → Privacy → Camera → Enable for RecruiterPro
   - Mac: System Preferences → Security & Privacy → Camera → Allow RecruiterPro
   - Linux: Check your system's permission manager

2. **Restart Camera:**
   - Unplug USB camera or disable built-in camera in settings
   - Wait 5 seconds
   - Plug back in or re-enable
   - Click **"Test Camera"** in Settings

3. **Update Drivers:**
   - Windows: Device Manager → Camera → Right-click → Update driver
   - Mac: Check for system updates (Software Update)

4. **Try Different Camera:**
   - If external camera, try built-in camera
   - Go to Settings → Audio/Video → Camera dropdown

#### 5.2 Microphone Not Working

**Issue:** Microphone detected but no audio

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Permissions** (same as camera above)
2. **Check Volume:**
   - Settings → Audio/Video → Microphone → Check input level
   - Speak into mic - level bar should move
   - Adjust volume slider

3. **Test with Recording:**
   - Click **"Test Microphone"** in Settings
   - Record 5 seconds
   - Play back to hear yourself

4. **Try Different Microphone:**
   - If external mic, try built-in mic
   - Or try headset mic if available

#### 5.3 Interview Freezes or Disconnects

**Issue:** Video/audio freezes or connection drops

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Internet:**
   - Open browser to test connection
   - Run speed test at speedtest.net
   - Need minimum 5 Mbps for HD video

2. **Reduce Video Quality:**
   - Go to Settings → Audio/Video
   - Lower resolution to 720p (from 1080p)
   - Lower frame rate to 24 fps (from 30)

3. **Restart Interview:**
   - If technical issue, apologize and restart
   - Most platforms allow resume from where you left off
   - Recording should continue

4. **Use Phone as Backup:**
   - If video fails, switch to audio-only interview
   - Call candidate's phone number
   - Many interviews can continue without video

#### 5.4 Recording Not Saving

**Issue:** Interview completed but recording not found

**Solutions:**
1. **Check Recording Status:**
   - During interview, red dot should appear (indicating recording)
   - At end, wait for "Recording saved" message

2. **Check Storage:**
   - Local disk full? Delete old interviews locally
   - Cloud account issue? Check internet connection
   - Wait a few minutes for upload to complete

3. **Check Recording Location:**
   - Go to Settings → Storage → Show Recording Folder
   - Files should appear there

4. **Contact Support:**
   - If still not found, contact support with interview ID
   - They can manually retrieve recording

#### 5.5 App Won't Launch

**Issue:** App crashes on startup or won't open

**Solutions:**
1. **Force Restart:**
   - Close app completely
   - Wait 10 seconds
   - Reopen

2. **Restart Computer:**
   - Sometimes fixes temporary issues
   - Close app, restart OS, reopen

3. **Reinstall App:**
   - Uninstall: Windows (Control Panel) / Mac (Drag to Trash)
   - Restart computer
   - Download and reinstall fresh copy
   - Log back in

4. **Check System Requirements:**
   - Windows 10+, Mac 10.14+, Ubuntu 18.04+
   - 4GB RAM minimum, 2GB free disk space
   - Webcam and microphone (USB or built-in)

## FAQ

### For Candidates

**Q: What should I wear for a video interview?**
A: Dress professionally, as you would for in-person interview. Solid colored clothing works best. Avoid patterns that strobe on camera.

**Q: What if I'm late to my interview?**
A: Contact the HR manager immediately through the email. Most interviewers are understanding of unexpected delays. Being more than 15 minutes late may result in rescheduling.

**Q: Can I reschedule my interview?**
A: Go to "My Interviews" and click "Request Reschedule" on the interview. Provide your preferred times. The HR manager will confirm new time within 24 hours.

**Q: Will my interview be recorded?**
A: If the job posting mentions proctoring/recording, yes. You'll be asked for consent before your interview. You can see which consents you've given in your profile settings.

**Q: How long before I hear back after my interview?**
A: Typically within 3-5 business days. You'll receive an email with the decision. If you don't hear back, check your "My Interviews" page for status.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes. Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. Note: This is permanent and can't be undone.

**Q: How is my personal data used?**
A: See our Privacy Policy (linked throughout the app). Your data is only used for the hiring process and is deleted according to our retention policies.

### For HR Managers

**Q: How many candidates can I import at once?**
A: Bulk import supports up to 10,000 candidates per file.

**Q: Can I customize the interview questions?**
A: Yes. Create custom templates in Settings → Interview Templates, or edit templates when scheduling.

**Q: How do I give interview feedback to my team?**
A: After interviews, add notes in the interview details. Schedule a calibration session to discuss top candidates with your team.

**Q: What happens if a candidate declines an interview?**
A: You'll be notified by email. You can contact them directly to reschedule or move to another candidate.

**Q: How long are interview recordings kept?**
A: By default, 90 days. Admin can configure retention period in system settings.

**Q: Can I export candidate data?**
A: Yes. Go to Applications → Select candidates → Export. Choose CSV or PDF format.

### For Administrators

**Q: How do I backup the system?**
A: Automated daily backups are performed. Go to Admin Panel → System → Backups to view backup history.

**Q: What's the maximum number of concurrent users?**
A: The system is designed for unlimited concurrent users, but specific limits depend on your server configuration.

**Q: How do I reset a user's password?**
A: Go to Admin Panel → Users → Select user → Reset Password. They'll receive an email link to set new password.

**Q: How long are audit logs kept?**
A: By default, 2 years. Configurable in Admin Panel → Settings → Data Retention.

**Q: Can I migrate data from another system?**
A: Contact support@recruiterpro.com for migration assistance. We offer data import services.
